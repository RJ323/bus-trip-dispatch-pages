This page describes how the [Bus Trip Dispatch] Bus Trip Dispatch system collects and uses information, including for SMS (text message) notifications sent to drivers.

What we collect

Driver name and phone number, entered by department staff into the dispatch roster. Trip details (date, time, pickup/dropoff location) are entered by staff when a trip is created.

How we use it

Phone numbers are used solely to send SMS notifications about trip assignments — new trip offers, confirmations, and status updates — to drivers on the department's active roster. Messages are dispatch-related only; this system does not send marketing or promotional messages.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on trip volume and is tied directly to dispatch activity, not a fixed schedule.

Opt-out

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts, or HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Since this system is used to assign work trips, a driver who opts out should notify a dispatcher directly so trips can still be assigned through another channel.

Sharing

Phone numbers and trip data are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except the service providers that operate this system on the department's behalf (SMS delivery via Twilio, scheduling via Google Calendar, data storage via Google Sheets, workflow automation via n8n).

Data retention

Roster and trip records are retained in the department's Google Sheet for as long as needed for dispatch operations and department recordkeeping, consistent with [department records-retention policy, if applicable].
